Preparation and Overview

 

 

 

Read and Follow All Safety Instructions

Read all safety precautions in the technical manual and on safety signs attached to this equipment. Failure to follow safety precautions could result in death or serious injury.

 

 

 

Lockout/Tagout Procedures

Power is required during portions of changeover. Follow your plant’s lockout/tagout procedures.

Cutoff knives are sharp. Keep hands clear. Be careful when working around the cutoff assembly. Follow lockout procedures before servicing.

Prior to changeover, clear all packages, containers, and carriers from the machine.

Tighten all knobs and locking handles after completing changeover adjustments.

After changeover is complete, verify machine setup by jogging the carrier and containers into the machine to achieve proper application and discharge of completed packages.

HMI Changeover Screens

Select Recipe

Follow these steps to select the desired recipe:

Push E-Stop at the operator panel.

Press Changeover on the HMI.

Using the arrows, select the desired recipe (1).

Press Start Changeover (2).

Follow the prompts on each of the Changeover screens and press Next Step until complete.

Some parts might be installed already and no action is required.
